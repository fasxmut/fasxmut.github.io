kjcpp::ixmax - kjcpp::imax

kjcpp::ixmax is the maxium signed integral class type. As kjcpp::ixmax is equivalent to kjcpp::ix64 in most cases, kjcpp::ixmax is thought of 64 bit, however kjcpp::ixmax and kjcpp::ix64 are two different types. kjcpp::ixmax supports all basic signed integral operators, bitwise operators, and object-oriented features.
