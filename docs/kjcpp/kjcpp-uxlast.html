kjcpp::uxlast - kjcpp::ulast

kjcpp::uxlast (kjcpp::ulast) is the last unsigned integral type of kjcpp::class_type. kjcpp::ulast is unsigned long long int, kjcpp::uxlast is kjcpp::class_type<kjcpp::ulast> .
